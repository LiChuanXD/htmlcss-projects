Channel Features

Uses original voices of this synthesizer, which is Piano, Fantasy, Violin, Flute, Guitar and ADSR to produce music in a harmonical way.

Technology

While the era is modernized, people produce music using soundboards and high technology keyboards. But we only used this synthesizer without circuit bending, this device is notable for its kitsch value among electronic musicians, due to its cheap construction and its unrealistic, unique low-fidelity(low-fi) sound. This technology also were ahead of its time for a synthesizer and artists sometimes still uses this ot produce music.

Power Source

Only uses battery or any AC power supply adapter. Very convenient.
