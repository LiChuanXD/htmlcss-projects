Gabe Logan Newell

An American Businessman that has huge influence in the video game industry.

Gabe Logan Newell is a Computer Programmer & businessman best known as the co-founder of the company Valve. He attended Harvard University in the early 1980s, but dropped out and worked for Microsoft as a producer for the early Windows operating systems.

While still working at the company, Gabe Newell and his co-worker Mike Harrington became convinced that video games were the future of entertainment after playing Doom and Quake. Intrigued by the prospect of having their own game development studio, Newell and Harrington left Microsoft and founded Valve.

Timeline of Gabe Newell's life and career:

  • 1962 - Born in Colorado, United States on November 3
  • 1956 - Attending Davis Senior High School in Davis, California
  • 1980 - Attended Harvard University
  • 1983 - Dropped out of Harvard University before graduating to work for Microsoft. For the next 13 years working at the company, serving as a producer of Windows 1.01, 1.02 and 1.03 operating systems. He stated that he learned more during the first 3 months at Microsoft than Harvard.
  • 1996 - Inspired by Michael Abrash, who left Microsoft and work on the computer game Quake, Newell and another Microsoft employee, Mike Harrington left the company and founded Valve on August 24. And Newell remains president until now. They used their money to fund Valve through the development of Half-Life and GoldSrc game engine.
  • 1996 - Gabe Newell is also married to Lisa Mennet on the same day he founded Valve
  • 1998 - Developed and launched Half-Life, which is one of the most famous video game in the world right now.
  • 2003 - Launched project Steam, which is a video game distribution service by Valve as a way to provide automatic updates for their games. And is enpanded to include games from third-party publishers, now Steam is one of the largest digital distribution platform in the world.
  • 2004 - Developed and launched Half-Life 2, which is a sequel of Half-Life story line.
  • 2006 - Gabe is suffered from Fuchs' dystrophy, and he had to get two cornea transplants.
  • 2010 - Forbes, an American business magazine, acknowledged Gabe Newell as "A Name You Should Know".
  • 2013 - March, Newell received the BAFTA Fellowship award for his contributions to the video game industries
  • 2017 - Forbes listed Gabe Newell among the 100 richest people in the United States, with an estimated net worth of US 5.5 Billion
